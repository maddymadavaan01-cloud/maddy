About God of War 🎮

The God of War franchise is a critically acclaimed action-adventure video game series created by Santa Monica Studio. It primarily follows Kratos, a Spartan warrior and demigod who embarks on brutal, mythic quests of vengeance and redemption, ultimately evolving from a vengeful Greek god into a protective Norse father

1. The Greek Era (The Vengeance Saga)The original saga follows Kratos as a servant of the Olympian gods. Tricked by Ares (the God of War) into murdering his own wife and daughter, Kratos breaks his servitude, kills Ares, and takes his place. After a betrayal by his father, Zeus, Kratos goes on a bloody crusade that results in the complete destruction of the Greek pantheon.

2. The Norse Era (The Redemption Saga)Years after the fall of Olympus, an older Kratos leaves his homeland and settles in the realm of Midgard (Norse mythology). Alongside his second wife, Faye, they have a son named Atreus. Following Faye's death, the games focus on Kratos and Atreus's journey to spread her ashes at the highest peak. Along the way, they become entangled in the affairs of the Norse gods, leading to the prophesied apocalypse of Ragnarök.
